📜 Rules and Regulations

  • All students must present a valid ID before entering the laboratory.
  • Students are only allowed to use the computers for educational purposes.
  • Food and drinks are strictly prohibited inside the laboratory.
  • Students must follow the proper shutdown procedure before leaving.
  • Any damage to equipment must be reported immediately to the lab technician.
  • Noise levels must be kept at a minimum to maintain a conducive learning environment.
  • Students must log out of their accounts after each session.
  • Unauthorized software installation is prohibited.
  • Students must respect the time limits for computer usage during peak hours.
  • Violation of rules may result in suspension of laboratory privileges.
